Min Gao

I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Yang Chen and Prof. Xin Wang. I received my M.S. degree in the College of Computer and Cyber Security at Fujian Normal University in 2021, advised by Prof. Li Xu.

My research interests include network science, high-order networks, social computing, machine learning, and fraud detection.
